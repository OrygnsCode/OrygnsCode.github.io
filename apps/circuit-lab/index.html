Circuit Lab Controls

  • Spawn: Click & Drag component from palette.
  • Connect: Use Wires to connect components.
  • Rotate: Double-click a component.
  • Split: Double-click a connection node.
  • Move: Drag component body or terminals.
  • Delete: Select & click Trash or press Delete.

Tip: Solid components (Battery, Resistor) must be connected via Wires!
